Enzyme Time! WebQuest and Video Game

Adapted from Ana Ulrich

Prior Knowledge: In this lesson, you will discover how enzymes help our bodies to break down or build up a specific substrate and catalyze chemical reactions. You will learn how pH, temperature, and concentration affect the rate of chemical reactions in our bodies, and will experiment using graphs and interactive games to learn and check your understanding of enzymes. Put your answers in a different color, please!

Learning Targets:

  • I can use a model to illustrate the 3-D structure of a protein and relate that structure to the biological function of an enzyme.
  • I can compare the activation energy of an uncatalyzed reaction with an enzyme-mediated reaction using a diagram.
  • I can investigate the factors that affect enzyme function and use that data to draw conclusions about the key components of enzyme functionality in living systems.
